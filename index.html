Problem Statement

Many Makerere University students struggle with emotional, spiritual, and mental issues. The lack of a consistent, safe, and faith_oriented platform to share prayer intetions or get guidance from priests or imams. This affects spiritual health and connection with God and Allah.

Problem Objectives - Building a secure app for prayer requests - Connect students with priests or imams - Support Christianity and islam distinctly - Store and retrieve prayer data securely using Firebase.

How the application works

the comeback App accepts user to register in the identities of ; - Islam i.e Imam and Student - Christian i.e priest and Student

Hyperlink

The application consists of : - Home page with the display of the religous leaders and their details - The Quran/Bible page for refferences and spiritual enrichment - The prayer request page for submission of the prayer point to the relious leader - The chat forum that enable the user to interract with other user on the application. This can be with identity or annoynmus - The profile page to display the pending prayer requests.

ALGORITHM

Prayer request This uses a greedy_style scoring algorithm, with the help of a balanced tree (SplayTreeMap) That fetches all religious leaders of the same religion. Each religious leader is scored based on; -preferrences -time since they were last assigned -number of requests they have had
