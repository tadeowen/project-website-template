Comeback is a mobile application developed to provide students at Makerere University with easy access to religious and spiritual support. The purpose of the app is to bridge the gap between students and religious leaders (priests) by offering an efficient, digital platform for submitting prayer requests, receiving spiritual guidance, accessing religious media, and engaging in one-on-one communication with priests.

The core objective of Comeback is to connect students with priests and imams. Its most innovative feature is the automated priest-user matching system, which intelligently assigns students to available priests based on their prayer requests. This system eliminates the delays and inefficiencies of manual scheduling. Additionally, the app access to a virtual holy bible and Quran for references to spiritual nourishment. Thus the application supports Christianity and islam distinctly plus storing and retrieving data securely using firebase.
